Assignment Scenario
Mia’s Sandwich Shop is a small local chain specialising in selling luxury handmade sandwiches to order. Mia is considering a ‘KIOSK style’ ordering and payment system for staff and customers allowing fast ordering and payment.  The kiosk will provide customers with an easy way to browse the menu, customise their order, and pay without needing staff assistance.

Mia has the following software requirements for the prototype ‘KIOSK style’ ordering system:

Graphical User Interface
Browsing of items by type/categories
Selection of items
Item shows picture, description, and price
Shopping cart and checkout screen
Production of receipt showing date, time, items, total paid
Options for add-ons and meal customisation
